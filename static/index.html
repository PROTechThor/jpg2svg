jpg2svg

Convert JPG/JPEG images to colored SVG. This is similar to Inkscape's Trace Bitmap function, but with the added feature of automatically tracing the image into multiple layers and applying color to each layer.

Try it out below. If you don't anything after clicking the convert button, then pick another image with more contrast. The closer a color is to white, the less likely will be picked up.
